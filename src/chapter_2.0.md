# Parts of Research Paper

- Title
- Abstract, List of keywords
- Introduction
    - Contribution - contains claims what is in the research paper
- Related Work/ Literary Review
- Methodology
    - Process of conducting research
- Body
    - Sections depending on your research
- Open Research Direction/Future Work/ Future Opportunities
    - Provides further ideas and directions in which further research can be done.
- Discussion
    - Present your views on the topic.
- Conclusion
    - What you have achieved and done.
- References
    - Citations form where you have added material in research.
    - No reference in abstract and conclusion.
